The Best Movies According to Eugene

My top 3 movies of all-time

The Fountain (2006)

This is my favourite movie, hands down. If you like art movies, this one would hit the spot.

Robocop (1987)

I've grown up on this movie. I could watch this another 100 times.

Mononoke Hime (1999)

The best anime I have ever seen. It is about the good and evil of humans, and their connection with spirits/nature.
